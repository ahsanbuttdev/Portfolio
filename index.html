Who I am

About Me

I'm a Data Analyst with hands-on project experience turning messy, complex datasets into clear narratives that drive smarter decisions. My work spans the full analytical pipeline — from sourcing and cleaning data to building interactive dashboards that non-technical stakeholders can actually use.

I work comfortably with Python (Pandas, NumPy), SQL & PostgreSQL, Tableau, Power BI, and Excel. I also have working knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which helps me communicate technical findings in polished, web-ready formats.

I'm genuinely curious about data and the stories it holds — whether that's uncovering a hidden trend in housing prices or mapping the spread of a global pandemic. I believe the best analysis is the kind that changes what you do next.
